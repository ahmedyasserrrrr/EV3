Launch

Launch the official Empire 7 website. List EVII on decentralized exchanges (DEXs). Build a strong community through social media campaigns and incentives. Achieve listings on CoinMarketCap (CMC) and CoinGecko to boost visibility.

Ecosystem Development (Q1-Q2 2025)

Here’s a step-by-step guide to purchasing EVII tokens:

Download Phantom Wallet

02

Buy Some Solana (SOL)
